How can I determine my skin type?

By watching how your skin feels and acts during the day, you may identify if it is oily, dry, combination, or sensitive.

How do I choose the right moisturizer for my skin type?

Consider whether you have combination, oily, or dry skin when choosing a moisturizer. Look for ingredients that are specific to your needs.

How do I create a skincare routine suitable for all seasons?

Use lightweight, protecting products in the summer and moisturizing products in the winter to adjust your routine to the changing seasons.

Are there specific skincare tips for sensitive skin?

Choose gentle, fragrance-free products; try new products on a tiny patch of skin; avoid toners with alcohol and abrasive exfoliants.

Are natural skin care products better than synthetic ones?

Depending on personal choice and skin sensitivity, either can be beneficial. There might be fewer synthetic additives in natural items.

What is the importance of using sunscreen daily?

Sunscreen lowers the risk of skin cancer and shields the skin from UV rays, which can cause sunburn and premature aging.
